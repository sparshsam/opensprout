# OpenSprout — AI Agent Instructions

## Product Identity

OpenSprout is a privacy-first plant care application. It helps users track care schedules, log activities, and maintain their plants.

## Rules

1. **Privacy-first.** Minimize data collection. No analytics or tracking.
2. **No accounts required.** Design for optional or guest access where possible.
3. **Calm UX.** Plant care should reduce stress, not add urgency.
4. **Local-first preference.** Data should work offline when possible.
5. **Branch naming:** \`feat/*\`, \`fix/*\`, \`docs/*\`, \`refactor/*\`, \`chore/*\`.
